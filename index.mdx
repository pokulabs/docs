---
title: "Welcome to Poku"
description: "The communication layer for AI agents: voice, SMS, WhatsApp, and Slack in one API."
---

Poku gives your agent a dedicated phone number to place calls and send texts to 47 countries.
We also give your agent the ability to loop in humans via WhatsApp, Slack, collect structured responses through forms, and bring that data back into context — all through a single API call.
